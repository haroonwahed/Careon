# CareOn Business OS v1.3

## 1. Positioning

CareOn monetizes **time-to-placement** and **coordination reliability** under scarcity — not seat-based “case ERP”.

**Buyer story (short):**

- Chain actors lose weeks matching manually; providers burn capacity on unclear requests; municipalities spend time on **compatibility** not on duplicating provider judgment.  
- CareOn offers a **neutral orchestration layer** with anonymous/pseudonymous matching, structured responses, and explicit financing/arrangement validation gates.

---

## 2. Value hypotheses

1. **Speed** — fewer round-trips to find acceptable capacity.  
2. **Clarity** — each actor sees **next action**, not a wall of status.  
3. **Trust** — uncertainty is explicit; audit trail is immutable.  
4. **Exit** — the platform celebrates **handoff** to systems of record; no lock-in narrative.

---

## 3. Non-markets (explicit)

- Replacing ECD clinical documentation.  
- Municipal ERP / finance system of record.  
- Permanent legal dossier for entire youth-care lifecycle inside CareOn.

---

## 4. Packaging principles

- **Land & expand** via operational teams first (aanmelders + providers), gemeente validation as gate — not as sole hero user in UX.  
- Pricing should align with **throughput** or **active orchestration** metrics, not “records stored forever”.

---

## 5. Risk register (business)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Perceived as surveillance | Constitution + copy discipline; purpose-bound signals only. |
| Over-promising AI finance | Arrangement hints framed as **advisory** + human confirmation. |
| Municipal politics on “who owns case” | Neutral orchestration narrative + clear exit / uitstroom. |
