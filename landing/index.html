Frequently asked questions

How do the AI workflows actually work?

You start with a template, customize the steps (research topics, set tone, add reviewers), and Verzio guides you through each phase. The AI assists with research, suggests improvements, and maintains brand consistency throughout.

Can I export to my existing channels?

Yes! Export as HTML, Markdown, or use our API integrations for WordPress, Webflow, and other popular platforms. You can also schedule direct publishing to social media channels.

How does team collaboration work?

Team members can collaborate in real-time with comments, suggestions, and approval workflows. Set up custom review processes where content automatically moves between writers, editors, and approvers.

What makes this different from other content tools?

Instead of just generating content, Verzio focuses on the entire workflow from ideation to publishing. It combines research, writing, editing, optimization, and distribution in structured, repeatable processes.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The free plan includes 3 projects and basic templates. Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You can cancel or downgrade anytime.
