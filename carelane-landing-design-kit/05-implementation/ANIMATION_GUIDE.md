# Motion guide

Motion should explain relationships, not decorate empty space.

## Allowed

- subtle route progress on scroll;
- small card elevation on hover;
- gentle node connection pulses;
- chart line reveal;
- section fade/translate of 8 to 16 px;
- CTA glow increase on focus or hover.

## Timing

- UI response: 140 ms
- Standard transition: 220 ms
- Section reveal: 420 ms maximum

## Restrictions

- no continuous floating cards;
- no parallax that harms readability;
- no animated gradients behind body text;
- no auto-playing complex product tours;
- no animation that blocks interaction.

Always provide a reduced-motion mode.
