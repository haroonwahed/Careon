# Landing page section specification

## 01 Navigation

**Goal:** orient visitors and provide direct access to a demo.

**Content:** logo, Platform, Voor wie, Werkwijze, Veiligheid, Over Carelane, Inloggen, Demo aanvragen.

**Behaviour:** transparent over hero initially; compact navy surface after scrolling. Mobile uses an accessible menu button and focus-managed drawer.

---

## 02 Hero

**Goal:** establish category, urgency and trust immediately.

**Headline:** `Betere beslissingen in schaarse zorg.`

**Support line:** Carelane helps municipalities and care providers coordinate the right care, at the right moment, through one explainable process.

**Primary CTA:** `Plan een demo`

**Secondary CTA:** `Bekijk hoe het werkt`

**Visual:** rebuild a product preview inspired by the reference. Show the Regiekamer, the five phases and next-best-action patterns. Do not ship the full reference image as the product UI.

**Trust strip:** `Veilig`, `AVG-bewust`, `Gebouwd voor de zorg`.

---

## 03 Care journey route

**Goal:** make the canonical process memorable.

Show exactly five connected phases:

1. Aanmelding
2. Matching
3. Aanbiederreactie
4. Plaatsing
5. Intake

Each phase gets:
- one sentence;
- responsible role;
- typical friction or proof of progress;
- semantic status colour.

Use a continuous route or signal line. This section should not look like five unrelated cards.

---

## 04 Regiekamer intelligence

**Goal:** demonstrate operational usefulness.

Show:
- attention today;
- bottlenecks;
- average lead time;
- ownership;
- one next-best-action row;
- a small trend chart.

Message: `Zicht op het geheel. Grip op wat nu nodig is.`

Do not make the section a generic KPI wall. Every metric must connect to an operational decision.

---

## 05 One case, multiple organisations

**Goal:** communicate coordinated work around one young person or case.

Place the case at the centre. Surround it with:
- municipality;
- coordinator;
- provider;
- client and family.

Show controlled information flow, decisions, feedback and placement preparation.

Avoid suggesting that all parties can see all data. Visibility is role-based and context-limited.

---

## 06 Explainable matching

**Goal:** demonstrate advice intelligence without presenting an autonomous decision-maker.

Show three provider options with:
- specialisation fit;
- availability;
- region/distance;
- relevant experience;
- constraints;
- match explanation.

One provider can be visually recommended. Include `Waarom deze match?` with concise trade-offs.

Primary CTA: `Bekijk matchredenen` or `Plan een demo`, depending on whether the section is interactive.

---

## 07 Trust by design

**Goal:** make governance tangible.

Five pillars:
- role-based access;
- workflow integrity;
- auditable decisions;
- data protection;
- controlled visibility.

Use architectural visuals, not padlock wallpaper. Explain what is controlled and why it matters.

Avoid unsupported certifications. Do not claim ISO, SOC 2 or other certification unless verified.

---

## 08 Results in practice

**Goal:** translate capabilities into outcomes.

Preferred outcomes:
- less waiting between steps;
- clearer ownership;
- fewer incomplete handovers;
- better capacity insight;
- faster decisions with preserved human control.

Use verified pilot data only. Until verified, display qualitative outcomes or explicitly labelled examples. No fictional customer names or testimonials.

---

## 09 Built for each role

**Goal:** clarify relevance without fragmenting the product.

Roles:
- municipalities;
- care providers;
- coordinators;
- clients and families.

Each role receives one concise outcome statement. Avoid four identical feature cards. Use a connected role map, tabs or a controlled sequence.

---

## 10 Final CTA

**Goal:** convert qualified visitors.

Headline: `Breng regie terug in de zorgketen.`

Primary CTA: `Plan een demo`

Secondary CTA: `Bekijk de werkwijze`

Include three low-friction assurances such as:
- korte kennismaking;
- afgestemd op uw organisatie;
- geen verplichtingen.

---

## 11 Footer

Use the purple illuminated bridge visual as the main thematic image: Carelane connects organisations while keeping the route controlled.

Include:
- product;
- for whom;
- resources;
- company;
- privacy and security;
- legal links;
- contact;
- copyright.

Do not show invented social accounts or inactive links.
