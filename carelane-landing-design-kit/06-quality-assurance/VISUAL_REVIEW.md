# Visual review procedure

Compare implementation and references at:
- 1440 × 1000
- 1024 × 900
- 768 × 1024
- 390 × 844

Review in this order:
1. overall rhythm and section order;
2. headline hierarchy;
3. hero composition;
4. five-phase route continuity;
5. operational clarity of Regiekamer;
6. human-centred network section;
7. explainability of matching;
8. restraint of trust section;
9. evidence integrity;
10. final CTA and bridge/footer;
11. focus states and reduced motion.

A section fails review when it looks attractive but does not communicate its operational purpose within five seconds.
