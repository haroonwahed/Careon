# Copy rules

## Voice

- Clear and specific
- Calm, respectful and professional
- Human without becoming sentimental
- Operational without bureaucratic jargon
- Confident without unsupported promises

## Prefer

- `Casus onvolledig`
- `Wacht op aanbiederreactie`
- `Klaar voor matching`
- `Plaatsing bevestigd`
- `Intake gepland`
- `Maak casus compleet`
- `Start matching`
- `Vraag gegevens op`
- `Plan intake`

## Avoid

- `AI-powered care revolution`
- `Slimme automatisering`
- `Workflow optimaliseren`
- `Bekijk aandacht`
- `Analyseer proces`
- `Genereer samenvatting`
- vague claims such as `transformeer de zorg`
- technical security claims that have not been verified

## Formatting

- Headings should be short
- Body copy should use sentences, not fragments piled into feature cards
- Maximum one dominant CTA per section
- Use Dutch as the primary language
- Do not mix Dutch and English except for a deliberately retained label such as `Trust by design`
