# CLM/CMS PLATFORM — PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## Project: Legal Consultancy CLM/CMS (EU + US Ready)

---

# 1. PRODUCT VISION

Build a **secure, AI-enabled Contract Lifecycle & Content Management System** for a legal consultancy that:

* Manages contracts, matters, and legal workflows end-to-end
* Supports **EU (GDPR)** and **US privacy frameworks**
* Provides **auditability, security, and compliance by design**
* Uses AI to **accelerate legal work without replacing human oversight**

---

# 2. TARGET USERS

### Primary Users

* Legal consultants
* In-house legal teams
* Contract managers
* Compliance officers
* Privacy officers (DPO)

### Secondary Users

* Clients (external portal)
* Finance / procurement teams
* External counsel

---

# 3. CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

* Security-first architecture
* Compliance-by-design (not bolt-on)
* Human-in-the-loop AI
* Metadata-driven system (not file-based)
* Modular and scalable
* Jurisdiction-aware (EU + US)

---

# 4. CORE MODULES (MVP REQUIRED)

## 4.1 Identity & Access Management

* SSO (SAML / OIDC)
* MFA (mandatory for admins)
* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Matter-level permissions
* Ethical walls / conflict restrictions
* Session management

---

## 4.2 Client / Matter / Entity Management

Entities:

* Client
* Matter / Project
* Legal Entity
* Counterparty

Features:

* Link contracts to matters and entities
* Ownership tracking
* Relationship mapping

---

## 4.3 Contract Repository (Core Engine)

Each contract must support:

### Metadata Fields

* Contract type
* Parties
* Governing law
* Jurisdiction
* Language
* Status
* Effective date
* Renewal date
* Termination notice
* Auto-renewal flag
* Value / currency
* Owner
* Risk level
* Data transfer flag (EU → US etc.)
* DPA / SCC attached (yes/no)

### Functional Requirements

* Upload + OCR
* Version control
* Immutable history
* Tagging system

---

## 4.4 Document Lifecycle Management

Workflow stages:

1. Drafting
2. Internal review
3. Negotiation
4. Approval
5. Signature
6. Execution
7. Obligation tracking
8. Renewal / termination
9. Archival

Requirements:

* Version comparison
* Redlining
* Comment threads
* Approval tracking

---

## 4.5 Approval Workflow Engine

Features:

* Conditional approval logic:

  * Based on value
  * Based on jurisdiction
  * Based on contract type
* Multi-step approvals:

  * Legal
  * Finance
  * Privacy
* Escalations and SLAs
* Delegation rules

---

## 4.6 Clause Library & Template Engine

Requirements:

* Pre-approved templates
* Clause variants per jurisdiction
* Fallback positions
* Negotiation playbooks
* Mandatory clauses (EU / US)
* DPA / SCC templates

---

## 4.7 Obligation & Deadline Tracking

Track:

* Renewal dates
* Termination notice windows
* Deliverables
* Compliance deadlines
* Data deletion obligations

Features:

* Automated reminders
* Dashboard overview
* Alerts

---

## 4.8 Search & Discovery

* Full-text search
* Metadata filtering
* Semantic search
* Clause search
* Saved filters

---

## 4.9 Audit Logging & Compliance Tracking

Track:

* User access
* Edits and approvals
* Downloads / exports
* AI interactions
* Deletions
* Data subject requests

Requirements:

* Tamper-evident logs
* Exportable audit reports

---

## 4.10 E-Signature Integration

* Integration with providers (DocuSign-like)
* Signature tracking
* Execution certificates

---

## 4.11 Privacy & GDPR Module

Features:

* Data inventory (RoPA support)
* Lawful basis tracking
* Retention policies
* Deletion workflows
* Legal hold
* DSAR workflows:

  * Access
  * Correction
  * Deletion
* Subprocessor registry
* Transfer tracking (EU ↔ US)
* SCC / DPF management

---

## 4.12 AI Services Layer (MVP)

### Capabilities:

* Contract summarization
* Clause extraction
* Metadata extraction
* Risk flagging
* Redline suggestions
* Document comparison

### Governance:

* Human approval required
* Confidence scoring
* Prompt logging
* No training on user data
* AI opt-out per client

# 5. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

## 5.1 Core Security

* Encryption at rest + in transit
* Tenant isolation
* Secrets management
* API authentication (token-based)
* Rate limiting

## 5.2 Advanced Security

* SIEM logging integration
* Vulnerability scanning (SAST/DAST)
* Backup & disaster recovery
* Malware scanning for uploads

## 5.3 Legal-Specific Security

* Ethical walls
* Conflict checks
* Privileged document flags
* Restricted export controls

# 6. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
